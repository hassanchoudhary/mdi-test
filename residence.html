INTRODUCTION

STAY AT THE MAS

The Mas des Infermières is located in the heart of Provence, in the Luberon Regional Natural Park. Situated in the “Golden Triangle” between Ménerbes and Gordes, in this magical area, surrounded by the vineyard, olive trees and truffle oak trees, you will find a little “hameau” of three different houses, created for the most perfect holiday. All three houses have been carefully renovated and refurbished, designed and furnished by Mr.Scott in a premium and contemporary

Provençal style. To make you feel truly at home, each house has its own private pool and garden, a fully equipped kitchen, vast living areas and all bathrooms as en suite. These charming houses can easily accommodate your family or group of friends, up to a party of six or nine. The Scott family welcomes you to their estate and hopes that, as they have, you fall in love with this quiet Provençal backwater.
